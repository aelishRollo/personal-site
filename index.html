Work

Useful software with a human reason behind it.

I have built tools for real workflows, small business websites, playful creative interfaces, and music-centered systems that turn theory into something a person can actually use.

Some of that work is about clarity: making a local business easier to understand, making an interface feel less scattered, or helping someone get to the next useful action. Some of it is about curiosity: building tools that understand chords, MIDI settings, and harmonic relationships, with little moments of personality that make software feel alive.
