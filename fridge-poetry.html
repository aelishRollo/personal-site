Interactive Lab

Fridge Poetry: drag magnets, build lines, and keep your layout saved.

Each word can only be used as many times as it appears in the source list. Magnet positions persist across sessions.

Embed This On Any Site

Use this snippet to embed the board in another page. You can point words-src at your own JSON or text source.

  storage-key="my-fridge-board"
