Interactive Lab

Fridge Poetry: drag magnets, improvise with words, and save your layout.

This is a playful writing tool inspired by musical improvisation: experiment, react, and build something in the moment.

Use This on Your Site

You can embed this board anywhere. Point words-src at your own vocabulary list and make it yours.

<script src="https://rollison.dev/assets/js/fridge-poetry.js" defer></script>

<fridge-poetry-board
  words-src="https://rollison.dev/assets/data/fridge-poetry-words.json"
  storage-key="my-fridge-board"
  cols="64"
  rows="24"></fridge-poetry-board>
