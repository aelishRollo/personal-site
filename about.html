About

I am a builder in two languages: code and music.

I am an Indianapolis native who loves turning ideas into shipped products, songs, and shared creative momentum.

The Through Line

I have been coding since high school, and I still love the full path from idea to implementation: planning, building, refining, and delivering something real.

Music gives me the same kind of energy. I love playing, recording, songwriting, mixing, producing, singing, and especially improvising with other people.

Both parts of my life come from the same instinct: make useful, expressive things with care, and make the process good enough that people want to keep creating together.
