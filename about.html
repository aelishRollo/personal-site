About

I am a builder in two languages: code and music.

I am an Indianapolis native who loves turning ideas into shipped products, songs, and shared creative momentum.

My Work in Tech

I have been coding since high school, and I still love writing code and solving real product problems. I love designing software that is optimized for performance and shaped by thoughtful UX, so it feels good to use.

I enjoy the full path from idea to implementation: planning, building, refining, and delivering.

My Work in Music

I love every part of music creation: playing, recording, songwriting, mixing, producing, and singing, and I am especially energized by improvisation with other people.

Jamming is one of my favorite ways to connect, experiment, and discover ideas that would not happen alone.

What I Am Looking For Right Now

  • Software teams that care about performance, UX quality, and thoughtful execution.
  • Music collaborators for playing, writing, recording, and production.
  • People in tech or music who enjoy building and sharing ideas together.
  • Conversations with anyone who wants to connect, trade ideas, or collaborate.

What You Can Expect

Clear communication, creative problem-solving, and follow-through.

What I Love Building

Software products, music projects, and hybrid ideas that sit between tech and art.

How I Collaborate

I am open to full-time roles, contract work, creative partnerships, and conversations of all kinds.
